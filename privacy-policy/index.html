Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2026

Welcome to Fridays ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • Username and display name
  • Profile photo (if you choose to upload one)

Content You Create: When you use Fridays, we collect:

  • Song posts you share (song name, artist, album information)
  • Interactions with other users' posts (favorites, shares, crowns)
  • Follow relationships between users

Usage Data: We automatically collect:

  • App activity and feature usage
  • Posting streaks and timestamps
  • Device information for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Fridays app
  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable social features (following, sharing, interactions)
  • Display your posts and profile to other users
  • Track your posting streaks
  • Send important service-related communications
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our service

Third-Party Services

Fridays integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication, database storage, and cloud functions. Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Music: We use Apple Music's API to search for and display song information. We do not access your personal Apple Music library or listening history.
  • Spotify: We use Spotify's API to provide links to songs. We do not access your personal Spotify account data or listening history.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With other users: Your profile, posts, and interactions are visible to other Fridays users as part of the app's social features.
  • Service providers: We share data with third-party services (listed above) that help us operate the app.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Access controls and security rules on our database

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information through the app
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

Fridays is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
