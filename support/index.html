Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fridays?

Fridays is a social music-sharing app where you can share your favorite song of the week with friends and discover what others are listening to.

How do I create an account?

Download Fridays from the App Store, open the app, and sign up with your email address. You'll need to verify your email before you can start using the app.

How do I post a song?

Tap the post button, search for a song using our music search, and share it with your followers. You can post one song per week to keep your picks meaningful.

What are streaks?

Streaks track how many consecutive weeks you've posted a song. Keep your streak alive by posting at least once every week!

How do I follow other users?

Search for users by their username and tap the follow button on their profile. Their posts will then appear in your feed.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the Settings page within the app. This will permanently remove your profile, posts, and all associated data.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password.
